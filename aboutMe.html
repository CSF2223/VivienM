Vivien Mogyoros's About Me

Character Traits

Cooperative

I listen to whatever others may say/suggest even if I do not agree. I believe that everyone is entitled to their own opinion and I will listen to whatever they wish to defend (as long as they attempt to defend it).

Creative

I love to write poetry, create music and analyze writing, both my own works and others.

Compassionate

I attempt to treat everyone how I wish to be treated and will listen to what others have to say (and take their opinion(s) into consideration.
